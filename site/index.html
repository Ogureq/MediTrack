What it does

Never guess when you're due again.

Know when you're due

Gemocode remembers when each blood test was last drawn and shows when it's commonly due again — so you skip the duplicate and never miss the overdue one. Always labeled "commonly recommended," never a fixed rule.

Scan any lab report

Photograph a printout or import the PDF. On-device text recognition finds the values and matches them against 46 common tests — with reference ranges, history charts, and plain-English explanations. Part of Premium; your first scan is free.

Type a sentence, it files itself

“bp 128/82” gets logged, “dentist tomorrow 3pm” gets logged — parsed on your phone, free, no AI. Premium adds AI entry for the messy version: one sentence with several items, filed all at once.

A recap every 90 days

Your Quarterly Review shows what changed, what improved, your streaks and goals — and the questions worth bringing to your doctor. Free forever.

AI that shows its work

Optionally, let AI narrate your review in plain language. It sees only a text summary — never your documents — and every number it says is verified against your data before you see it. Your first scan and report are free, together, once.

The part nobody else can say

What leaves your phone

Gemocode has no accounts and no cloud. This isn’t a policy promise — it’s the architecture. Here is the complete outbound data ledger:

Your dataSent to us
Lab reports, photos & PDFs0 bytes
Vitals, medications, symptoms0 bytes
Your name, email, identity0 bytes — there’s no sign-up
Analytics & tracking0 bytes
Optional AI report (you tap the button)a text summary, never files

Everything lives in your iPhone’s storage, protected by your passcode and Face ID. Backups are encrypted files only you hold. Delete the app and your data is gone — we couldn’t keep it if we wanted to.
